Welcome to Adfree Review!


AdfreeReview is a service for internet users who search internet for online reviews, which consists of a Chrome extension and a website. Users can check scores of blog reviews and rate the posts' adfree-ness and helpfulness with Chrome extension. Website curates the top adfree-ness blogs and posts based on the scores users rated and each user's preference.

By showing blog review scores rated by other users on the top of the blog review, users can decide whether to consult the review or not.
